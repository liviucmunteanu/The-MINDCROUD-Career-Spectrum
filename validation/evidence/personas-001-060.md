# MINDCROUD Career Spectrum — Validation Personas 001–060

Instrument facts used in persona design: 5-point Likert (1=Strongly disagree … 5=Strongly agree); 6 Career Avatars (MAK Maker/Realistic, EXP Explorer/Investigative, VIS Visionary/Artistic, MEN Mentor/Social, PIO Pioneer/Enterprising, GRD Guardian/Conventional); 60 scored items (10 per avatar; 12 reverse-scored) across facets ACT/ENV/NRG; attention checks Q17 (Agree), Q33 (Strongly disagree), Q49 (Neutral); consistency pairs Q41~Q1 (teaching) and Q56~Q10 (detail-checking); output = Spectrum Scores 0–100, 2–3 Dominant Avatars, 15 pair-combination profiles.

Template per persona:
- **Persona [ID]: [Name]** — Age | Gender | Background | City, Country | Education
- Career: role, industry, experience/stage
- Big Five: E / C / A / N / O (Very Low–Very High)
- Drivers: motivational drivers + behavioral pattern
- PURPOSE: what this persona stress-tests
- TENDENCY: expected response pattern (incl. reverse items, checks, consistency pairs where relevant)
- OUTCOME: expected Dominant Avatars (or flag / flat profile)
