# PART 2: THE 120-PERSONA BANK

## PERSONA PROFILE FORMAT

Each persona includes:
- **ID & Name** (fictional)
- **Demographics**: Age, gender, cultural/ethnic background, country of residence, education
- **Career Context**: Current role, industry, years of experience, career stage
- **Psychological Profile**: Big Five approximation, key motivational drivers, known behavioral patterns
- **Testing Purpose**: What this persona is designed to test or reveal about the instrument
- **Expected Response Tendencies**: Brief note on how this person would likely approach the assessment
